<p>Here is a <a href="https://open.spotify.com/playlist/1JtPZ9nphLL7nt5S4PFyke?si=8e181f3f38f04374">playlist of classical music recontextualized</a> into different genres or styles. Another <a href="https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLQm-O-sCwRHNsAjhVw-rHqVn2FiDFgK8M&si=aokw3L9NA3d8rDLs">playlist of funny fugues and quasi-fugues</a> with modern or bizarre themes.</p>

However, I will always root for the South Korea national football team in every World Cup they play in. These games were probably the best <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rR7mo8yT4mk">9 minutes</a> and <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UaIRKgFxsdw">7 minutes</a> of my life as a South Korean, apart from the 2002 games that I was too young for.</p>
